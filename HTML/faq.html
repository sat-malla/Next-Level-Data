FAQ

What is this?

Here are some frequently asked questions about my website. If you have a different question, you can contact me through here.

To fill up a form, you can go to our forms section or click this. Then, answer each question and once you are done you can submit. Remember, the question or the forms are not required to be completed. You do what you want to do.

The slides in the Data section are data that I collected before I made this website. Your response won't appear in these slides, but you can get a feel of what these forms are about. If you go to the data section and scroll down to the last section, you can see data from the forms created in this website. If you want to be part of it, go ahead and fill up a form!

The forms will not close in this website. You are welcome to fill up a form anytime you want.

When you submit a form, it will go to a google sheet. The google sheet will store that data, but the google sheet in the live data won't update yet unfortuanetely. It may take some time for the data to appear in the live data section. But don't worry, it will show up soon.
